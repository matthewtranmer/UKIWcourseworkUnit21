United Kingdom Inland Waterway (UKIW) Marinas PLC is a new company setup by our founder, John Davids. It has recived large investments from the financial community aswell as the investing public.

John Davids is a major sharholder in MDL Marinas, Safe Harbor Marinas and many other companies; who created UKIW marinas to build his own marina company from the bottom up.

UKIW marinas is a big supporter of the canal river trust. Click here to go to the canal and river trust webpage.
